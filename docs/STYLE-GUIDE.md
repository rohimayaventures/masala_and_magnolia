# Style Guide

## Global Style & Voice Requirements
- Tone: warm, intimate, descriptive, and family-forward.
- Use present-tense when describing recipes and procedural steps.
- Keep cultural notes respectful, contextual, and specific.

## Required Recipe Format
- Title + Cultural Tag
- Times (prep, cook, total)
- Ingredients listed in cups + grams (ordered to match steps)
- Step-by-step instructions with technique cues
- Timeline Cue, Troubleshooting, Variations, Storage, Pairings, Family Note, Beginner Walkthrough

## Mini Essays Placement
- Mini essays live at the end of each part or as transition pieces between phases; mark them with H2 and a motif icon.

## File Naming Rules
- Kebab-case or lowercase with underscores permitted for phase folders; keep filenames predictable: `part-#_slug.md`.
